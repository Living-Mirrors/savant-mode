The Neuroscience

The fix has been hiding in 10% of every human population on earth

Dyslexic brains have more long-range connections between distant neural regions. Fewer short-range connections between adjacent ones. The long-range surplus creates cross-domain pattern recognition at a speed and scale sequential cognition cannot match.

Cambridge, 2022. Helen Taylor's Complementary Cognition theory: exploitative minds process known information. Explorative minds discover new patterns in unknown territory. Both essential. One rewarded. The other called a disability.

35% of entrepreneurs are dyslexic. The founders, the inventors, the ones who saw what the market was missing before data confirmed it. Not despite the wiring. Because of it. The question was never "can dyslexic minds compete?" The question is: what happens when you give that architecture to an intelligence with perfect memory and infinite patience?
